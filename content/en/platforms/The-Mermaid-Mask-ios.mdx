<Section icon={Map} title="Full Walkthrough: Prologue & Chapter One">

The story begins at the Stonetop Inn before transitioning to the main setting: the Mortuga submarine. Follow this route to ensure you don't miss any critical evidence required for the final confrontation.

**Prologue: The Arrival**
- **Stonetop Inn**: Inspect the red **Case File** and the book *The Hex of the Helmsmen*. Wake Sally to proceed.
- **Town Square**: Inspect the glowing green light area. Speak to Sally to trigger the submarine's surfacing.
- **Fore Deck**: Board the ship and enter the Parlor. Activate the lever to restore lighting.
- **Mess Hall**: Solve the Snake Cabinet puzzle (3-9-7-4) to unlock the path to the Illusion Theatre.
- **Illusion Theatre**: Inspect the crime scene. Examine the **Stone Cauldron**, the chalk outline, and the **Ragged Sail Cloth**.

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Milestone">
Completing the first deduction screen—concluding that Magnus Mortuga was killed by something inside the cauldron—officially begins **Chapter One: The Curse in the Cauldron**.
</Callout>

**Chapter One: The Main Investigation**
- **Child's Room**: Solve the portrait puzzle to obtain the **Glass Eyeball**. Use it to unlock the Captain's Cabin later.
- **Control Room**: Meet Sinthia Seafoam. Solve the switchboard (1-9, 3-11, 6-12) to get the **Captain's Log**.
- **Bunk Rooms**: Unlock Dirk's phone and find the **Inn Deeds** under Sinthia's mattress.
- **Cargo Hold**: Interview Mariana Moon. Once you meet all 8 suspects, complete the **Crew Location Pinboard**.
