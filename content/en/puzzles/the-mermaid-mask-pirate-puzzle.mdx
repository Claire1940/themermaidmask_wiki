<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solution for the Mermaid Mask Pirate Puzzle">

With all three mask fragments in your inventory and the crew's testimonies recorded in your journal, return to the captain's quarters. The central console is a complex mechanical device that requires precise inputs. The alignment of the masks corresponds to the legendary voyage of the Mermaid's Grace, a historical pirate ship. According to the lore, the ship sailed toward the setting sun carrying gold, braved the freezing northern winds with silver, and fired its bronze cannons toward the southern horizon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Insert the Mask Fragments">
    Approach the central display board and place the Gold, Silver, and Bronze mask fragments into their corresponding circular slots. The slots are color-coded, so ensure they match perfectly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rotate the Dials">
    Rotate the outer dial of the console clockwise until the compass needle points North. This aligns the internal gears with the submarine's current heading.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Align the Masks">
    Rotate each mask to face its designated cardinal direction based on the suspect testimonies. Turn the Gold Mask West, the Silver Mask North, and the Bronze Mask South.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Insert the Rusty Pirate Key">
    Insert the Rusty Pirate Key into the keyhole beneath the central compass. Turn the key counterclockwise to release the locking mechanism and open the hidden compartment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If done correctly, the steam valves behind the console will hiss, and the metal door to the safe will slide open. If the lights flash red, it means one of the masks is misaligned. Double-check the direction of the Gold Mask, as players often mistake the western alignment for the eastern starting point due to the mirrored console design.

<Callout type="info" title="Console Layout">
The console is mirrored relative to the submarine's bow. Keep this in mind when determining West and East on the control board.
</Callout>
