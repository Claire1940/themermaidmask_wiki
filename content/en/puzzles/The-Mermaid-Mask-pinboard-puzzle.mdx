<Section icon={Target} title="Solving The Mermaid Mask Pinboard Puzzle">

Resolving the board requires a systematic approach. Follow these steps to organize your thoughts, connect the correct nodes, and solve the puzzle without getting lost in the web of lies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Initial Evidence">
    Collect the captain's logbook from the desk, the bloody wrench from the lower engine room deck, and the torn letter from the crew quarters wastebin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Map Suspect Alibis">
    Place all suspect cards on the board. Draw yellow strings from each suspect to their respective claimed locations during the time of the murder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify Contradictions">
    Compare the captain's logbook with Miss Seafoam's statement. Connect them using a blue string to highlight the discrepancy in the navigation logs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Final Deduction">
    Link the bloody wrench to the engine room, then connect the engine logs to the true culprit to unlock the final confrontation scene.
  </Step>
</Steps>
