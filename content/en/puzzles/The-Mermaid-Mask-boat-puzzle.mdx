<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Puzzle Solution">

Once you have gathered the Brass Compass, Valve Schematic, and Logbook Fragment, return to the engine room at the back of the vessel. Follow these steps carefully to solve the puzzle and open the hatch.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Relieve Steam Pressure">
    Locate the three steam valves on the left wall of the engine room. Refer to the Valve Schematic to adjust the pressure. You must rotate Valve A two full turns clockwise, Valve B one and a half turns counter-clockwise, and Valve C three full turns clockwise.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Align the Navigation Compass">
    Interact with the central console. Place the Brass Compass into the circular indentation. Rotate the dial until the needle points to 45 degrees Northeast, matching the coordinates engraved on the back of the compass.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Decode the Captain's Logbook">
    Open the console's input panel. Refer to the Logbook Fragment to enter the correct date code. The code corresponds to the date Magnus Mortuga took command of the submarine. Input the numbers sequence: 0-7-1-8.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Engage the Main Lever">
    Once the pressure gauges stabilize in the green zone and the compass lock clicks, pull the heavy iron lever on the right side of the console. The hatch leading to the lower deck will swing open.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Valve | Direction | Rotations | Target Pressure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Valve A | Clockwise | 2 Full Turns | 40 PSI |
| Valve B | Counter-Clockwise | 1.5 Turns | 65 PSI |
| Valve C | Clockwise | 3 Full Turns | 90 PSI |

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
When successfully executed, the steam gauges will glow green, and a distinct metallic unlocking sound will play. You can now safely enter the lower deck.
</Callout>

| Symptom | Probable Cause | Recommended Solution |
|---|---|---|
| Red Light Flashing | Pressure is too high | Release Valve A by a half turn to bleed excess steam |
| Lever Jammed | Compass is misaligned | Reset the needle to zero and realign to 45 degrees |
| Hatch Locked | Out of sequence | Restart from Step 1 and verify logbook code entry |
