### How to Solve the Key Mechanisms

- **Portrait Cards & Lenses**: In the Child's Room, combine the six cards to form three complete girls. Place Patricia in the center with her medium-sized doll. Use the magnifying and shrinking lenses on the left and right dolls, then place the fish-eye lenses over their faces to secure the **Glass Eyeball**.
- **Control Room Switchboard**: Connect the colored cables to restore power. Connect the red cable from socket 1 to 9, the blue cable from socket 3 to 11, and the yellow cable from socket 6 to 12. This opens the compartment containing the **Captain's Log**.
- **Pink Pig Safe**: In the Women's Bunk Room, arrange the numbers on the safe's branching pathways. The top branch must read 3, 4, 1 (totaling 8), and the second branch must read 2, 3, 1 (totaling 6). Align the bottom totals to 5, 9, and 2 to retrieve the **Gloomy Underwater Photograph**.
- **Four Compasses**: In the Engine Room, inspect the compass mechanism. Set the yellow compass to point left, the blue compass to point down, the pink compass to point toward two o'clock, and the red compass to point toward the upper-left (Northwest). This yields the **Repaired Compass**.

### Utilizing Mariana's Dictaphone Recordings

A major turning point in Chapter One involves finding the **Pipe-Shaped Dictaphone** in the Cargo Hold. Present this suspicious object to Mariana Moon after gathering mechanical evidence from the Engine Room. This unlocks several secretly recorded conversations between the crew. 

Listen to every available recording in your inventory, then present these statements to the involved characters to expose contradictions in their alibis.
