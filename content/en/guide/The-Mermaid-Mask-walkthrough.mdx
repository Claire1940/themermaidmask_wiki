<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Solving the Serpent Statue Puzzle">

One of the primary mechanical hurdles in **The Mermaid Mask walkthrough** is the large metal serpent statue located within the submarine. This statue contains a built-in cabinet with a complex locking mechanism involving four dials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Statue">
    Find the metal serpent statue near the transition between the drawing room and the kitchen area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Examine the Dials">
    Interact with the cabinet to reveal four dials, each with a window in the center showing different shapes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Align the Shapes">
    Rotate the dials to match the shapes based on environmental clues found in the "Hex of the Helmsman" or the harbor fountain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Cabinet">
    Once the correct sequence is entered, the cabinet opens to reveal further clues regarding Magnus Mortuga.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The puzzle requires a keen eye for detail. The serpent itself is a recurring motif, appearing as both a stone fountain in the town and a metal statue on the ship. Note the debate between Heat and Sally regarding whether a creature with legs can truly be called a "serpent."

| Dial Position | Shape/Symbol | Connection |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dial 1** | Serpent Head | Related to the fountain statue |
| **Dial 2** | Ship/Vessel | Related to the Dearest Selena |
| **Dial 3** | Waves/Ocean | Related to the maritime theme |
| **Dial 4** | Mystery Symbol | To be determined by case file notes |

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Strategy">
If you are stuck on the dial puzzle, revisit the fountain at the harbor. The description of the "Silkworm Serpent" wrestling with a fisherman provides the necessary context for the symbols required to unlock the cabinet.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Illusion Theater Crime Scene">

The climax of the demo occurs in the Illusion Theater. This is where Magnus Mortuga was found dead. The room is remarkably small, essentially a dead-end corridor partitioned by a door. The "locked-room" nature of the murder is the central hook of **The Mermaid Mask walkthrough**.

**Crime Scene Observations:**
- **The Body**: Magnus Mortuga landed on his back with a cauldron at his feet.
- **The Wound**: A clean cut across the neck, yet no weapon was found in the locked room.
- **The Cauldron**: A mysterious container that was reportedly never unlocked before the incident.
- **The Furniture**: A knocked-over stool that looks identical to the bar stools at the Stone Top Inn.

| Clue | Observation | Potential Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Broken Bottle** | Found on the floor | Dropped by Mortuga during the struggle |
| **Upside-down Poster** | Taped to the wall | Possible rebranding of the room |
| **Scratch Marks** | Inside the cauldron | Suggests something was trapped inside |
| **Blood Trail** | Leading to the body | Indicates the point of attack |

The Illusion Theater itself seems to be a recent "rebranding" of a standard utility room. The presence of a cloth hanging on the wall and a banner with "Illusion Theater" painted on it suggests the room was staged for a specific purpose or performance before the murder occurred.

<Callout type="warning" title="The Cauldron Mystery">
The cauldron is the most unsettling element. Despite appearing empty, the scratch marks on the inside suggest that Mortuga may have unlocked it and been killed by "what was inside," despite the physical impossibility of a person hiding in such a small container.
</Callout>
