<Section icon={Target} title="Solving the Serpent Statue Dial Puzzle">

As you descend into the Mess Hall, you are blocked by a large metal statue of a serpent. This statue contains a built-in cabinet that is locked by a mechanical dial puzzle. To progress in **The Mermaid Mask guide**, you must align four geometric symbols with their corresponding numbers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Examine the Hint">
    Look at the top right of the puzzle screen to see the required arrangement of shapes and numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Rotate the Dials">
    Interact with the four dials to move the Square, Triangle, Circle, and Diamond into place.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confirm the Positions">
    The Square must be on 4, the Triangle on 3, and the Circle/Diamond on their respective points as shown in the hint.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect the Reward">
    Once aligned, the cabinet opens to reveal a bottle of "Sun Tablets."
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Symbol | Target Number | Item Found |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Square** | 4 | Brown glass bottle |
| **Triangle** | 3 | Sun Tablets (Z-shaped) |
| **Circle** | 9 | Happy/Sunshine pills |

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
Solving this puzzle is mandatory to move deeper into the submarine. The Sun Tablets found here will be compared against the Moon Vial found later at the crime scene.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Search} title="Crime Scene Analysis: The Illusion Theater">

The Illusion Theater is where the tragedy occurred. The room is small, cramped, and was found locked from the inside. Following the trail of blood leads you to the body of Captain Mortuga, lying on his back in front of a massive stone cauldron.

**Evidence Collected:**
- **Moon Vial**: A smashed glass bottle on the floor containing a purple liquid with a botanical scent.
- **Sail Cloth**: A thick piece of silk cloth found hanging on the wall.
- **Stargazing Girl Banner**: A rebranding of the theater, formerly known for different performances.
- **The Cauldron**: An ancient stone container with deep scratches on the inside.

| Evidence Item | Location | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Smashed Moon Vial** | Near the body | Labeled with a moon; spilled purple liquid. |
| **Stone Cauldron** | At Mortuga's feet | Marked with barnacles and internal scratches. |
| **Upside-down Poster** | Taped to the wall | Advertising "Lady of the Looking Glass." |
| **Blood Trail** | Floor | Leads from the neck wound toward the back of the room. |

<Callout type="info" title="The Cauldron Mystery">
The case file states the cauldron has never been unlocked before, yet it was found open at the crime scene. The internal scratches suggest something—perhaps something with claws—was trapped inside.
</Callout>
