<Section icon={Target} title="Puzzle Solutions and Mechanical Guides">

The submarine is rigged with various mechanical locks and puzzles that block your progress. Solving these is mandatory for advancing the story.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Serpent Statue Dial">
    In the Mess Hall, find the metal serpent statue. Rotate the four dials to match the sum of the edges. For example, the number 7 requires a triangle (3) and a square (4) to be aligned. This unlocks a bottle of Vitamin D tablets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Child's Room Box">
    Examine the wooden chest with cards. Arrange the lenses and cards so that the dolls appear the correct size. Patricia's doll must be neither too large nor too small. This reveals a strange glass eye.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Bilge Light Puzzle">
    Use Dirk's cell phone as a flashlight in the dark Bilge. Locate the dial on the wall. Rotate the metal dials and glass orbs until only two white orbs are visible. This reveals Madam Tadpole's license.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Engine Room Map">
    In Mr. Grip's shop, solve the compass alignment puzzle. Identify the "Clock in the Skies" and the compass that points away from others. This unlocks a secret scroll showing a red 'X' in the ocean.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
Unlocking the cell phone in the bunk room requires overlapping the digital tiles perfectly. Focus on matching the curved edges in the top row first to clear the lock.
</Callout>
