<Section icon={Shield} title="Beginner Tips to Optimize Your Playtime">

If you want to keep your playthrough efficient without sacrificing the rich story, follow these proven strategies to avoid common progression blocks:

- **Rotate 3D Clues Completely**: Whenever you collect a physical object, rotate it a full 360 degrees. Zoom in on the base, back, and any visible seams. Many clues, like the Glass Eyeball or the repaired compass, contain vital details hidden on their reverse sides.
- **Exhaust Suspect Dialogues**: When meeting a suspect for the first time, ask every introductory question. Revisit them whenever you find personal items, such as the Inn Deeds for Sinthia or the letter from Ibis for Wirman.
- **Use the Notebook Frequently**: The notebook is your best tool for tracking active lines of inquiry. If you are unsure where to go next, look for entries that still lack a definitive conclusion.
- **Listen to the Dictaphone**: Once you unlock Mariana's pipe-shaped dictaphone, listen to every recording. These private conversations expose critical contradictions that are required to unlock advanced personal investigations.

<Callout type="info" title="Progression Block Solution">
Most apparent dead ends are caused by a single unread clue reaction or an incompletely inspected 3D object. Before backtracking through the entire submarine, re-examine your newest inventory items.
</Callout>
