<Section icon={Target} title="The Mermaid Mask Walkthrough: The Curse in the Cauldron">

The investigation is divided into distinct chapters, beginning with a prologue that sets the stage for the primary mystery. Your objective is to move through the submarine, unlock restricted areas like the Control Room, and establish a timeline of events. The "Curse in the Cauldron" chapter is where the meat of the investigation occurs, requiring you to meet all eight primary suspects and collect the evidence necessary for the final confrontation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Prologue Investigation">
    Start at the Stonetop Inn. Inspect the red Case File and the book titled *The Hex of the Helmsmen*. After waking Sally, head to the Town Square and examine the unusual green light. Board the Mortuga submarine once it surfaces. Inside, move to the Mess Hall to solve the snake cabinet puzzle before entering the Illusion Theatre to examine the initial crime scene.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking the Control Room">
    In Chapter One, navigate to the Child's Room. Solve the portrait card mechanism to obtain the Glass Eyeball. Use this item to meet Symmetry Silkmoth and eventually gain access to the Control Room. Here, you will meet Sinthia Seafoam and recover the Captain's Log by solving the switchboard cable puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Suspect Interviews and Evidence Collection">
    Systematically visit the Crew Quarters, Dream Chamber, Cargo Hold, and Engine Room. You must interview all eight suspects: Sinthia, Symmetry, Dirk, Wirman, Zephyr, Mariana, Gripp, and Madam Tadpole. Present every new piece of evidence, such as the Gloomy Underwater Photograph or the Inn Deeds, to trigger new dialogue and contradictions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Reconstruction">
    Once all personal investigations are complete in the notebook, return to the Illusion Theatre. You must build the final timeline using proven conclusions. Present the matching physical evidence for each deduction prompt to reveal the culprit and the truth behind the Mermaid Mask.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Essential Puzzle Solutions: Master the Mortuga Mechanisms">

The puzzles in **The Mermaid Mask steam next fest** and the full game rely heavily on visual logic and mechanical interaction. Unlike traditional point-and-click games that use abstract riddles, these puzzles are integrated into the environment and the objects you collect. Below are the solutions for the most common roadblocks encountered during the first half of the game.

| Room | Puzzle Name | Solution Summary |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mess Hall** | Snake Cabinet | Set dials to 3 (triangle), 9 (square+pentagon), 7 (triangle+square), 4 (square). |
| **Child's Room** | Portrait Cards | Place Patricia center/medium doll. Use fish-eye lenses on left/right girls. |
| **Control Room** | Switchboard | Connect Red 1 to 9, Blue 3 to 11, and Yellow 6 to 12. |
| **Bunk Room** | Pink Pig Safe | Top branches: 3, 4, 1 (Total 8). Second row: 2, 3, 1 (Total 6). |
| **Engine Room** | Four Compasses | Yellow: Left. Blue: Down. Pink: 2 o'clock. Red: Upper-left. |
| **Airlock** | Diving Helmet | Rotate dials so every pipe route starts and ends with the same symbol. |

<Callout type="info" title="Investigation Hint">
The "Crew Location Pinboard" in the Cargo Hold only becomes solvable after you have met every living crew member. If you are missing a portrait, you need to explore the remaining rooms first.
</Callout>
