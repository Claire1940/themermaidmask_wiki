<Section icon={Anchor} title="Boarding the Moruga Submarine">

The Moruga is not a standard vessel. Its arrival is preceded by a green light emitted from a buoy connected by a chain to the submarine's deck. As the tide shifts or the ship surfaces, the buoy acts as a lure. Upon boarding, you will notice the name "Seafoam" has been crossed out and replaced with "Moruga," hinting at a possible mutiny or a sudden change in ownership before the tragic events occurred.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Buoy Lure">
    Follow the green light to the harbor. The light fades as you step onto the deck, indicating the vessel's readiness for your arrival.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Sail Entry">
    Enter through the "sail" or "fairwater" (the tower of the submarine). The door is left open, suggesting someone recently entered or exited.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Descending the Ladder">
    Move into the pitch-black interior. Use the lever at the bottom of the ladder to light the candles and illuminate the upper deck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Parlor Investigation">
    Explore the drawing room. Note the earthy smell of soil, which is out of place for a metal vessel submerged in salt water.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazard">
The submarine interior is poorly maintained. Note the "earthy" smell in the parlor; this environmental clue suggests that something other than sea water has been brought aboard.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Search} title="The Crime Scene: Illusion Theater">

The room is unusually small for a theater, essentially a repurposed corridor separated by a heavy curtain. The scene is static but contains several high-density clues that players must analyze to progress the narrative.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Key Mechanics and Interaction Guide">

Interacting with the world in the **The Mermaid Mask demo** is straightforward but requires a keen eye for detail. The game uses a point-and-click style navigation combined with a map system for movement. Every object highlighted by the cursor can potentially trigger a conversation between Seao and Sally, which is essential for uncovering the "Case File" details.

| Action | Control/Method | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Movement** | Map button (Bottom Left) | Transition between rooms/areas |
| **Inspection** | Click on objects | Triggers dialogue and clues |
| **Lighting** | Wall levers | Toggles candles or pink lights |
| **Case File** | Inventory/Menu | Review known facts and objectives |
