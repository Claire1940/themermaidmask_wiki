**The Deduction System:**
- **Sentence Building**: Players drag blocks of text and images into empty spaces to form a logical accusation.

- **Brute Force Protection**: While you can guess, the game uses a "thumbs up/down" logo system to guide you, though it encourages genuine logical thinking.
